## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is fiber video surveillance?
Fiber video surveillance uses fiber optic cabling to transmit video data from cameras to monitoring and recording systems over long distances with high performance and reliability.

### Can fiber video surveillance support high-resolution cameras?
Yes — fiber networks easily handle IP, HD, and 4K video streams without degradation.

### Is fiber video surveillance suitable for industrial environments?
Yes — Versitron’s fiber video solutions include rugged components designed for challenging conditions.
